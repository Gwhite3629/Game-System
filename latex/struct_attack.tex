\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A struct that defines an attack. 

Attacks are defined by a name, damage amount, and damage type. Damage values are before armor and regardless of attack type. \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_types}{Types}} are used to calculate resistances and weaknesses.
